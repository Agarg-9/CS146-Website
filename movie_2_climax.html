Climax


Release Date: March 1, 2019
Studio: A24
Genre: Drama
Run time: 1hr 37min
Rated: R

When members of a dance troupe are lured to an empty school, drug-laced sangria causes their jubilant rehearsal to descend into a dark and explosive nightmare as they try to survive the night -- and find out who's responsible -- before it's too late.

Reviews

Lily Haynes:
This one is … something. It’ll be seen as pretentious by some people, others might
see it as a unique way to tell a story. It feels really claustrophobic given the
subject matter, especially towards the end. The lighting and production design is
really cool, but the main standout of this movie is the choreography. If you like
20 minute dance scenes, this movie’s for you.
